In the 'global village' of today, everything assumes global dimensions; everything is interconnected.

Express Student Services is global educational institution recruiter for many colleges in UK, Canada, and New Zealand. Education is no exception.

ESS is an educational recruiter of distinction, combining excellence with innovation. ESS places a high value on providing its clients with a fulfilling educational experience in their chosen discipline. An international qualification equips students not only with vital skill sets, but also a global mindset - one that broadens the mind and allows students to easily slide into the multiethnic, multilingual workplaces of global corporations.

The need of the times is the presence of good market facilitators who enable students and professionals to seek quality education beyond the borders.

On the one hand, there are a large number of Indians seeking an overseas degree; on the other hand, there are foreign educational institutions eyeing Indian students as possible intake. Even then only a minuscule proportion of the total student body in India actually ends up going abroad for studies every year. Clearly there is a need for better interfaces between potential students in India and education providers elsewhere in the world.

This is where we come in. The ESS works at improving awareness of foreign courses, colleges, destination countries, admission formalities, immigration procedures, providing help thereof and assuring strong support on foreign shores for Indian students or professionals and delivering greater penetration on Indian soil for foreign institutions.

I am confident that you will find a pathway to a bright future in a chosen career or profession.
